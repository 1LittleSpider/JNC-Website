About Us

Establishment

Founded in 2006, we have been on a remarkable journey in the field of indoor gardening. It all started with Mrs Chen, a life-loving woman who adores home gardening and adorning her home. With an indoor garden always blooming in her heart, she initiated this indoor planting business. Joining her is Professor Xu, a renowned photobiologist from Nanjing Agricultural University, sharing the same passion for blending home coziness with nature.

Product Portfolio

Our diverse product range includes Kitchen Grow, Window Grow, Shelf Grow, Corner Grow, and Tabletop Grow. These products are designed to fit various home spaces, allowing for flexible indoor planting.

Mission

Our goal is to bring nature closer to people within the comfort of their homes. By enabling them to grow their own healthy, fresh, and pollution-free vegetables, we aim to let everyone experience the pure joy of planting.

Founders

Mrs Chen: She is a life-loving woman who adores home gardening and creating cozy, nature-filled spaces. Her passion drives our team to deliver innovative indoor gardening solutions.

Professor Xu: A renowned photobiologist from Nanjing Agricultural University, he leads a team of experts in horticulture, agriculture, lighting, and aesthetics, constantly innovating to simplify the planting process.

Market Reach

Our products are well-received in key markets across Europe, North America, Japan, and Australia, demonstrating the global appeal of our indoor gardening solutions.
